Short summary: I am a Professor of Theoretical Astrophysics at the University of Birmingham, UK. Until 2011, I was an NSF Astronomy & Astrophysics Postdoctoral Fellow, collaborating with Scott Hughes at MIT, and a member of Vicky Kalogera's research group at Northwestern University. Previously, I was a graduate student in Kip Thorne's group at Caltech, and an undergraduate research assistant at the Gravity Probe B project at Stanford. More details are available in my CV.

My current research focuses on gravitational-wave astrophysics. For more information, please see my research publications.
